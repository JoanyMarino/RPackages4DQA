ExPanD - Explore your data!

Welcome to ExPanD! To start exploring your data, please upload a data file containing at least two numerical variables. For panel data, the data needs to be in long format and without duplicate observations. Currently supported formats are Excel, CSV, RData, RDS, STATA and SAS.
